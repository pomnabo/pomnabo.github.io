Important Question
Below are the three starter Pokemon of the Kanto region: Bulbasaur, Charmander, and Squirtle. Choosing your starter Pokemon is the most important decision that will guide the trajectory of your journey in the Pokemon world. You can choose your new companion based on its looks, its type advantages and abilities, its evolution chain; the factors are endless! I think the best method is to choose which you like best, regardless of how it performs in battle.

Bulbasaur

A strange seed was planted on its back at birth. The plant sprouts and grows with this Pokémon. For some time after its birth, it grows by gaining nourishment from the seed on its back

Charmander

From the time it is born, a flame burns at the tip of its tail. The flame on its tail indicates Charmander's life force. If it is healthy, the flame burns brightly. Its life would end if the flame were to go out.

Squirtle

The surface of Squirtle's shell help minimize resistance in water, enabling this Pokémon to swim at high speeds. It shelters itself in its shell, then strikes back with spouts of water at every opportunity.

Who will you choose?
