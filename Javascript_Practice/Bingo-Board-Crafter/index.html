Bingo Board Crafter

Instructions:
  • Use the dropdown menu to select the number of boxes you want for your bingo card(s).
  • Type your bingo card prompts into the text box below. Use a plus sign "+" before each prompt to indicate a new box item. You must provide the minimum number of prompts to fill the board. You may also provide additional prompts beyond the maximum for more unique cards.
  • Toggle the switch on or off to apply a fixed "Free Space" box in the center of your board. You may also write your own custom "free space" prompt as a line break item.
  • When finished, click the "generate" button to create your randomized bingo card. Click the button again to generate alternative randomized placements. Your bingo card will generate in a new window.
  • You can click the printer icon to save your board as an image file, or the download icon to save as a pdf.
