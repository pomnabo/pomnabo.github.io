Bingo Board Crafter

Instructions:
  • Choose your bingo board size.
  • Toggle the switch to apply a fixed "Free Space" box in the center of your board.
    * You may also type your own custom "free space" in the text area below as its own box item.
  • Type your own text for the free square (optional).
  • Give your bingo card a title.
  • Type your bingo prompts into the text box below. Use a dash "-" before each prompt.
    * You must provide the minimum number of prompts to fill the board.
    * You may also provide additional prompts beyond the maximum for more unique cards.
  • Click the "generate" button to create your randomized bingo card.
  • You can click the printer icon to save your board as an image file, or the download icon to save as a pdf.
